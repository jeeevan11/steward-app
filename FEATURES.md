# What Steward can do — the full list, in plain words

Think of Steward as a chief of staff for your messages. Here's everything it does.

### It watches everything, so you don't have to
- Reads your **WhatsApp and email** all day, every day.
- Understands the **whole conversation**, not just the latest line.

### It clears the noise
- Newsletters, promotions, spam, automated notices → **filed away quietly.** You never see them.
- At the end of a busy day it might say *"handled 900+ things quietly — you saw the 4 that mattered."*

### It drafts your replies — in your voice
- For messages that need an answer, it **writes the reply for you**, sounding like *you*.
- You just read it and tap **Send**. You never type a thing.
- Works for both **WhatsApp and email**.

### It never acts behind your back
- **It never sends a message without your approval tap.** Ever.
- **It never quietly handles something important.** Money, investors, big decisions, anything that
  needs *your* judgment → it **flags it and waits for you**, instead of guessing.

### It's aware of everything you do
- Reply to someone **yourself on your phone**? It notices and **clears that from your list** — no
  double replies, nothing left hanging.
- It knows when your message was **delivered** and **read** — so it can say *"they read it 2 hours
  ago and haven't replied yet."*
- It can nudge you about a message that **didn't go through** ("this didn't deliver").

### It learns and remembers
- Learns **who matters to you** — important people always get surfaced.
- Remembers **what you promised people** ("you said you'd send the deck") and reminds you.
- Builds a quiet memory of your relationships so its judgment gets better over time.

### It knows who's who — and lets you fix it when it doesn't
- Recognizes the people you've **saved** (phone-book and WhatsApp-verified names) so it never
  treats a real contact as a stranger.
- When someone is genuinely **unknown**, it says so plainly instead of guessing — an
  **"Unknown" chip** appears on the card and in **People**.
- One tap **"Save contact"**: give them a name (and, for WhatsApp, an optional phone number).
  That links their WhatsApp ID to the number, so every future message is recognized too.
  Saving only updates who-is-who — it **never sends anything**.

### It respects your attention
- If you're **already handling a chat yourself**, it stays quiet about it.
- You can mark people as **VIP** (always tell me) or **mute** (handle quietly) — but even a muted
  person still gets surfaced if they suddenly say something that really matters (money/legal).
- **Quiet hours** so it doesn't nudge you in the middle of the night.

### It shows you the picture
- A clean **menu-bar app** on your Mac: what needs you, what it handled, time it saved you, the
  people it knows, the promises you've made.
- Or just use **Telegram** — cards come to your phone, you tap to approve.

### It's private and safe
- Runs **on your own computer**. Your messages and keys **never leave your Mac.**
- Starts in **dry-run mode** (drafts everything, sends nothing) until *you* decide to go live.
- Works with **any AI provider** — bring your own key (OpenRouter, OpenAI, and more).
