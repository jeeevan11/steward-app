# PERF_PROFILE.md

Phase 9 performance profile of Steward's scaling-critical, DB-bound paths, seeded to a realistic multi-year volume on a WAL SQLite file. Latencies in milliseconds. Generated by `scripts/profile_pipeline.py`.

**Scale:** 10,000 pending_actions, 50,000 learning_events, 40,000 prunable rows.

| Operation | Scale | P50 | P90 | P99 | Max |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| live-queue render  get_queue() | 10,000 rows | 0.73 | 0.80 | 1.08 | 1.15 |
| dashboard  learning_summary() | 50,000 events | 120.07 | 121.89 | 164.78 | 194.86 |
| dashboard  metrics_accuracy() | 50,000 events | 8.47 | 8.67 | 8.81 | 8.94 |
| settle planner  plan_settling() | 1,000 msgs | 0.78 | 0.83 | 0.97 | 1.05 |
| ledger  mark_seen() (dedup gate) | single | 0.02 | 0.02 | 0.05 | 2.04 |
| retention.prune() (one pass) | 40,000 del | 26.8 | — | — | 26.8 |

## Index ON/OFF — live-queue render at scale (scaling-time-1/3)

| idx_pa_message_id + idx_learning_events_ts_type | P50 | P90 | P99 |
|---|---|---|---|
| WITH indexes | 0.74 | 0.82 | 0.94 |
| WITHOUT (pre-fix) | 16.43 | 17.91 | 21.81 |

**Speedup from the added indexes: ~22.3x at P50** (16.43ms → 0.74ms).

## Notes

- LLM/network stages (classification, drafting, transcription) are network-bound and not benchmarkable offline; they are governed by LLM_COST_BOUNDED (daily cap + 429 breaker + media byte cap), verified in `test_llm_layer_hardening`.
- `plan_settling` is the only material local compute on the WhatsApp path and is sub-millisecond at 1,000 queued messages.
- retention now batches deletes and checkpoints the WAL (storage-persistence-7 / scaling-time-5), so a large first prune no longer takes one giant lock.
